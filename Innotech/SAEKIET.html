Related Information

The journey of SAEINDIA Collegiate Club of KIET started with the participation of Team IMMORTALS of KIET Group of Institutions in BAJA SAEINIDIA 2009. The Team was awarded with the position of first runner up and won a prize money of total 2 lac rupees. In 2009, the team also participated in BAJA SAE Alabama. The team IMMORTALS was the only team from Asia who participated in BAJA SAE Alabama 2009. The year 2013 was the memorable year for the team immortals when the team Immortals won overall 2nd rank in the competition winning a cash prize of Rs.3 lacs.
